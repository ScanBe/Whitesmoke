Shortcuts

class="[\w|\W]*?"

Select all instances (or in selection) of class/href/attribute within element

Used for Ctrl+F to search/replace all attribute="any value" pairs within the selected, or all, elements.

\n\n

Used to remove empty lines when replaced with \n

Used for Ctrl+F to search/replace all empty lines by replacing \n\n with \n.

Use regular expressions in the search/replace tool.
